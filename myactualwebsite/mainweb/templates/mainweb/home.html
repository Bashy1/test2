About Us

Here at SBC our goal is to make your cloud journey as low fuss as possible. We aim to ensure that our solutions achieve key business needs around: service resliency, ease of use and cost. We use strong relationships with all the main UK carriers and best of breed vendors in order to ensure the highest quality of service to our customers.

SBC is committed to enabling digital transformation for you and your business. Priding itself on honesty, customers can expect clear and cogent responses to their questions. There is a fundamental beleive that trust and transparency through all aspects of the relationship are key to mutual growth and collective success. We never over-complicate the solutions and always strive to be easy to work with.

All of our services are delivered via interconnects with tier 1 carrier networks and key vendor peering agreements so that we can provide tangible end-to-end solution delivery. Resiliency is built in at the application layer and into the core network architecture.

What does this mean for our customers?

The output will vary depending on your own desired business outcomes and the solution that is delivered. At its most base level it means that our service uptime is carrier grade, and even in the event of a disaster scenario at our customer premises, key cloud services can continue to run and remain accessible for all - minimising any disruption to you and your business. Using access to all the main carriers also allows SBC to be carrier agnostic, choosing whichever one fits the bill the best in any given scenario.

SBC strives to put the solution first, and work backwards towards the most suitable vendor relationships. Whether for use of BT's hosted centrex, SlverPeak's SD-WAN or Wavenet's Microsoft TeamsLink platform.

As part of working with us, you will be actively involved in helping steer the solution's direction through mutual communication and will become viewed not just as a customer but a business partner. This strategic relationship is a fundamental part of ensuring that SBC takes on the companies which we can work best.

SBC's contracts are all on 12 month terms for customers. Pricing will be outlined alongside any solutions discussions and, in fitting with principles of transparency only include an 8% mark-up over costs. This is to strengthen customer trust and ensures that we are able to deliver the best possible prices for solutions sold.
